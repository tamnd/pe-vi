**The Ackermann function**

For non-negative integers m, n, the Ackermann function A(m, n) is defined as follows:

<center><img src="https://projecteuler.net/project/images/p282_formula.gif"></center>

For example A(1, 0) = 2, A(2, 2) = 7 and A(3, 4) = 125.

Find $\Sigma^6_{n=0}A(n,n)$ and give your answer mod 14<sup>8</sup>.
***
## **Hàm Ackermann**

Hàm Ackermann: $A(0,n) = n+1$; $A(m,0) = A(m-1,1)$; $A(m,n) = A(m-1, A(m, n-1))$.

Tính $\sum_{n=0}^{6} A(n,n) \pmod{14^8}$.
