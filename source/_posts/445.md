***
**Retractions A**

For every integer n&gt;1, the family of functions f<sub>n,a,b</sub>  is defined by f<sub>n,a,b</sub>(x)≡ax+b mod n for a,b,x integer and  0&lt;a&lt;n, 0≤b&lt;n, 0≤x&lt;n.
We will call f<sub>n,a,b</sub> a retraction if f<sub>n,a,b</sub>(f<sub>n,a,b</sub>(x))≡f<sub>n,a,b</sub>(x) mod n for every 0≤x&lt;n.
Let R(n) be the number of retractions for n.

You are given that
∑ R(c) for c=C(100 000,k), and 1 ≤ k ≤99 999 ≡628701600 (mod 1 000 000 007).
(C(n,k) is the binomial coefficient).
 
Find ∑ R(c) for c=C(10 000 000,k), and 1 ≤k≤ 9 999 999.
Give your answer modulo 1 000 000 007.
***
## **Rút lui A**

$f(n)$ là số hàm $f:\{1,\ldots,n\} \to \{1,\ldots,n\}$ thỏa $f \circ f = f$. Tính $f(10^{14}) \pmod{10^9}$.
