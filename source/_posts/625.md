## **Gcd sum**

$G(N)=\sum_{j=1}^N\sum_{i=1}^j\text{gcd}(i,j)$.
You are given: $G(10)=122$.

Find $G(10^{11})$. Give your answer modulo 998244353.

***
## **Tổng GCD**

$G(N) = \sum_{j=1}^{N} \sum_{i=1}^{j} \gcd(i,j)$. Tính $G(10^{11})$.
