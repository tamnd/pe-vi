# [Problem 461](https://projecteuler.net/problem=461)
***
**Almost Pi**

Let f<sub>n</sub>(k)&nbsp;=&nbsp;e<sup>k/n</sup>&nbsp;-&nbsp;1, for all non-negative integers k.

Remarkably, f<sub>200</sub>(6)&nbsp;+&nbsp;f<sub>200</sub>(75)&nbsp;+&nbsp;f<sub>200</sub>(89)&nbsp;+&nbsp;f<sub>200</sub>(226)&nbsp;= <u>3.1415926</u>44529…&nbsp;≈&nbsp;π.

In fact, it is the best approximation of π of the form f<sub>n</sub>(a)&nbsp;+&nbsp;f<sub>n</sub>(b)&nbsp;+&nbsp;f<sub>n</sub>(c)&nbsp;+&nbsp;f<sub>n</sub>(d) for n&nbsp;=&nbsp;200.

Let g(n)&nbsp;=&nbsp;a<sup>2</sup>&nbsp;+&nbsp;b<sup>2</sup>&nbsp;+&nbsp;c<sup>2</sup>&nbsp;+&nbsp;d<sup>&thinsp;2</sup> for a, b, c, d that minimize the error: |&nbsp;f<sub>n</sub>(a)&nbsp;+&nbsp;f<sub>n</sub>(b)&nbsp;+&nbsp;f<sub>n</sub>(c)&nbsp;+&nbsp;f<sub>n</sub>(d)&nbsp;-&nbsp;π|
(where |x| denotes the absolute value of x).

You are given g(200)&nbsp;=&nbsp;6<sup>2</sup>&nbsp;+&nbsp;75<sup>2</sup>&nbsp;+&nbsp;89<sup>2</sup>&nbsp;+&nbsp;226<sup>2</sup>&nbsp;=&nbsp;64658.

Find g(10000).
***
## **Gần pi**

$f(n) = \min_{1 \le a,b,c,d \le n} |a/b + c/d - \pi|$. Tính $\sum_{n=1}^{1000} f(n) \times 10^{10}$.
