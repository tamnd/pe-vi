**Modulo Summations**

Let a<sub>n</sub> be a sequence recursively defined by: $a\_1=1, a\_n=(\sum_{k=1}^{n-1}k·a_k) \text{ mod } n$.

So the first 10 elements of a<sub>n</sub> are: 1,1,0,3,0,3,5,4,1,9.

Let f(N,M) represent the number of pairs (p,q) such that: 

$$ 1 \le p \le q \le N \text{ and } (\sum_{t=p}^{q}a_i) \text{ mod } M=0$$

It can be seen that f(10,10)=4 with the pairs (3,3), (5,5), (7,9) and (9,10).

You are also given that f(10<sup>4</sup>,10<sup>3</sup>)=97158.

Find f(10<sup>12</sup>,10<sup>6</sup>).
***
## **Tổng modulo**

$a_1=1$, $a_n = (\sum_{k=1}^{n-1} k \cdot a_k) \bmod n$. $f(N,M)$ là số cặp $(p,q)$ với $1 \le p \le q \le N$ và $\sum_{t=p}^{q} a_i \equiv 0 \pmod M$.

Tính $f(10^{26}, 10^6)$.
