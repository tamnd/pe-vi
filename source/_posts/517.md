**A real recursion**

For every real number $a>1$ is given the sequence $g\_a$ by:

$g\_a(x)=1$ for $x<a$

$g\_a(x)=g\_a(x-1)+g\_a(x-a)$ for $x\ge a$

$G(n)=g\_{\sqrt{n}}(n)$

$G(90)=7564511$.

Find $\Sigma G(p)$ for $p$ prime and $10000000<p<10010000$

Give your answer modulo $1000000007$.
***
## **Truy hồi thực**

$f(x) = x + 1/(2x)$ cho $x > 0$. Xây dựng $a_n$ bắt đầu từ $a_0$. Tính $f(10^{18})$.
