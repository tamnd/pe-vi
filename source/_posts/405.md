**A rectangular tiling**

We wish to tile a rectangle whose length is twice its width.
Let T(0) be the tiling consisting of a single rectangle.
For n &gt; 0, let T(n) be obtained from T(n-1) by replacing all tiles in the following manner:

<center><img src="/resources/images/0405_tile1.png"></center>

The following animation demonstrates the tilings T(n) for n from 0 to 5:

<center><img src="/resources/images/0405_tile2.gif"></center>

Let f(n) be the number of points where four tiles meet in T(n).
For example, f(1) = 0, f(4) = 82 and f(10<sup>9</sup>) mod 17<sup>7</sup> = 126897180.

Find f(10<sup>k</sup>) for k = 10<sup>18</sup>, give your answer modulo 17<sup>7</sup>.
***
## **Lát gạch hình chữ nhật**

$f(m,n)$ là số cách lát gạch $m \times n$ bằng gạch $2 \times 1$ và $L$-tromino. Tính $f(10^{12}, 10^{12}) \pmod{10^8+9}$.
