**Divisibility of Harmonic Number Denominators**

The n<sup>th</sup><b>harmonic number</b> H<sub>n</sub> is defined as the sum of the multiplicative inverses of the first n positive integers, and can be written as a <b>reduced fraction</b> a<sub>n</sub>/b<sub>n</sub>.
$H_n = \displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^n \frac{1}{k} = \frac {a_n} {b_n}$, with$\text {gcd}(a_n, b_n)=1$.

Let M(p) be the largest value of n such that b<sub>n</sub> is not divisible by p.

For example, M(3) = 68 because $H_{68} = \frac{a_{68}}{b_{68}} = \frac{14094018321907827923954201611}{2933773379069966367528193600}$,
b<sub>68</sub>=2933773379069966367528193600 is not divisible by 3, but all larger harmonic numbers have denominators divisible by 3.

You are given M(7) = 719102.

Find M(137).
***
## **Chia hết của mẫu số số điều hòa**

$H_n = \sum_{k=1}^n 1/k = p_n/q_n$ tối giản. Đếm $n \le 10^7$ sao cho $q_n$ chia hết bởi một số nguyên tố $> n^{0.8}$.
