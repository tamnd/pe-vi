**Red, green, and blue tiles**

Using a combination of black square tiles and oblong tiles chosen from: red tiles measuring two units, green tiles measuring three units, and blue tiles measuring four units, it is possible to tile a row measuring five units in length in exactly fifteen different ways.

<center><img src="/resources/images/0117.png"></center>

How many ways can a row measuring fifty units in length be tiled?

NOTE: This is related to [Problem 116](/Problem101-125/#Problem_116).

## **Gạch đỏ, xanh lá và xanh dương**

Có bao nhiêu cách lát hàng dài $50$ đơn vị dùng kết hợp ô đen, gạch đỏ ($2$), xanh lá ($3$) và xanh dương ($4$)?
