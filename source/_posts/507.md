**Shortest Lattice Vector**

Let $t\_n$ be the tribonacci numbers defined as:
$t\_0=t\_1=0$;
$t\_2=1$;
$t\_n=t\_{n-1}+t\_{n-2}+t\_{n-3}$ for $n≥3$
and let $r\_n=t\_n \text{ mod } 10^7$.

For each pair of Vectors $V\_n=(v\_1,v\_2,v\_3)$ and $W\_n=(w\_1,w\_2,w\_3)$ with 
$v\_1=r\_{12n-11}-r\_{12n-10},v\_2=r\_{12n-9}+r\_{12n-8},v\_3=r\_{12n-7}·r\_{12n-6}$ and 
$w\_1=r\_{12n-5}-r\_{12n-4},w\_2=r\_{12n-3}+r\_{12n-2},w\_3=r\_{12n-1}·r\_{12n}$ 
we define $S(n)$ as the minimal value of the manhattan length of the vector $D=k·V\_n+l·W\_n$ measured as 
$|k·v\_1+l·w\_1|+|k·v\_2+l·w\_2|+|k·v\_3+l·w\_3|$
for any integers $k$ and $l$ with $(k,l)≠(0,0)$.

The first vector pair is (-1, 3, 28), (-11, 125, 40826).
You are given that $S(1)=32$ and $\Sigma^{10}\_{n=1}S(n)=130762273722$.

Find $\Sigma^{20000000}\_{n=1}S(n)$.
***
## **Vector lưới ngắn nhất**

Vector lưới $2D$ ngắn nhất không song song với trục. Tính $\sum_{k=1}^{10^7} f(k)$ với $f(k)$ là độ dài vector lưới ngắn nhất cho lưới $k \times k$.
