**Totient Chains**

Let φ be Euler's totient function, i.e. for a natural number n, φ(n) is the number of k, 1 ≤ k ≤ n, for which gcd(k,n) = 1.

By iterating φ, each positive integer generates a decreasing chain of numbers ending in 1.
E.g. if we start with 5 the sequence 5,4,2,1 is generated.
Here is a listing of all chains with length 4:

&nbsp;5,4,2,1
&nbsp;7,6,2,1
&nbsp;8,4,2,1
&nbsp;9,6,2,1
10,4,2,1
12,4,2,1
14,6,2,1
18,6,2,1

Only two of these chains start with a prime, their sum is 12.

What is the sum of all primes less than 40000000 which generate a chain of length 25?
***
## **Chuỗi hàm totient**

Đặt $\varphi$ là hàm totient Euler. Lặp $\varphi$ tạo ra chuỗi giảm dần kết thúc tại $1$.

Tìm tổng tất cả số nguyên tố $p < 4 \times 10^7$ có độ dài chuỗi $\varphi$ bằng $25$.
