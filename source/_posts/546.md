**The Floor's Revenge**

Define $f_k(n)=\sum^{n}_{i=0}f_k(\lfloor \frac{i}{k} \rfloor)$ where $f_k(0)=1$ and $\lfloor x \rfloor$ denotes the floor function.

For example, f<sub>5</sub>(10) = 18, f<sub>7</sub>(100) = 1003, and f<sub>2</sub>(10<sup>3</sup>) = 264830889564.

Find $(\sum^{10}_{k=2}f_k(10^{14})) \text{ mod } (10^9+7)$.
***
## **Trả thù sàn nhà**

Dãy $a_n$: $a_0 = 1$, $a_n = \lfloor n \cdot a_{n-1} \rfloor + 1$ hoặc $\lfloor n \cdot a_{n-1} \rfloor$. Tính $a(10^{14})$.
