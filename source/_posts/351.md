**Hexagonal orchards**

A <i>hexagonal orchard</i> of order n is a triangular lattice made up of points within a regular hexagon with side n. The following is an example of a hexagonal orchard of order 5:

<center><img src="/resources/images/0351_hexorchard.png"></center>

Highlighted in green are the points which are hidden from the center by a point closer to it. It can be seen that for a hexagonal orchard of order 5, 30 points are hidden from the center.

Let H(n) be the number of points hidden from the center in a hexagonal orchard of order n.

H(5) = 30. H(10) = 138. H(1 000) = 1177848.

Find H(100 000 000).
***
## **Vườn hình lục giác**

Lưới tam giác trong lục giác đều bậc $n$. Đếm các điểm nhìn thấy được từ trung tâm (không bị che khuất).

Tính $H(10^6)$.
