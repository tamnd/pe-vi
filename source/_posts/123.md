**Prime square remainders**

Let p<sub>n</sub> be the n<sup>th</sup> prime: 2, 3, 5, 7, 11, ..., and let r be the remainder when (p<sub>n</sub>?1)<sup>n</sup> + (p<sub>n</sub>+1)<sup>n</sup> is divided by p<sub>n</sub><sup>2</sup>.

For example, when n = 3, p<sub>3</sub> = 5, and 4<sup>3</sup> + 6<sup>3</sup> = 280 ≡ 5 mod 25.

The least value of n for which the remainder first exceeds 10<sup>9</sup> is 7037.

Find the least value of n for which the remainder first exceeds 10<sup>10</sup>.
***
## **Số dư bình phương nguyên tố**

Tìm $n$ nhỏ nhất mà số dư khi $(p_n-1)^n+(p_n+1)^n$ chia cho $p_n^2$ lần đầu vượt $10^{10}$.
