**Four Representations using Squares**

Consider the number 3600. It is very special, because

<center>3600 = 48<sup>2</sup> + &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp;36<sup>2</sup>
3600 = 20<sup>2</sup> + 2×40<sup>2</sup>
3600 = 30<sup>2</sup> + 3×30<sup>2</sup>
3600 = 45<sup>2</sup> + 7×15<sup>2</sup>
</center>

Similarly, we find that 88201 = 99<sup>2</sup> + 280<sup>2</sup> = 287<sup>2</sup> + 2×54<sup>2</sup> = 283<sup>2</sup> + 3×52<sup>2</sup> = 197<sup>2</sup> + 7×84<sup>2</sup>.

In 1747, Euler proved which numbers are representable as a sum of two squares.
We are interested in the numbers n which admit representations of all of the following four types:

<center>n = a<sub>1</sub><sup>2</sup> + &nbsp;&nbsp; b<sub>1</sub><sup>2</sup>
n = a<sub>2</sub><sup>2</sup> + 2 b<sub>2</sub><sup>2</sup>
n = a<sub>3</sub><sup>2</sup> + 3 b<sub>3</sub><sup>2</sup>
n = a<sub>7</sub><sup>2</sup> + 7 b<sub>7</sub><sup>2</sup>,
</center>

where the a<sub>k</sub> and b<sub>k</sub> are positive integers.

There are 75373 such numbers that do not exceed 10<sup>7</sup>.
How many such numbers are there that do not exceed 2×10<sup>9</sup>?
***
## **Bốn cách biểu diễn bằng tổng hai bình phương**

Số $3600$ có thể biểu diễn theo bốn dạng $a^2 + kb^2$ với $k = 1, 2, 3, 7$.

Đếm số nguyên $\le 2 \times 10^9$ có thể biểu diễn theo tất cả bốn dạng đó.
