**Subsequence of Thue-Morse sequence**

The <b>Thue-Morse sequence</b> {T<sub>n</sub>} is a binary sequence satisfying:
- T<sub>0</sub> = 0
- T<sub>2n</sub> = T<sub>n</sub>
- T<sub>2n+1</sub> = 1 - T<sub>n</sub>

The first several terms of {T<sub>n</sub>} are given as follows:
01101001<span style="color:red;">10010</span>1101001011001101001....

We define {A<sub>n</sub>} as the sorted sequence of integers such that the binary expression of each element appears as a subsequence in {T<sub>n</sub>}.
For example, the decimal number 18 is expressed as 10010 in binary. 10010 appears in {T<sub>n</sub>} (T<sub>8</sub> to T<sub>12</sub>), so 18 is an element of {A<sub>n</sub>}.
The decimal number 14 is expressed as 1110 in binary. 1110 never appears in {T<sub>n</sub>}, so 14 is not an element of {A<sub>n</sub>}.

The first several terms of A<sub>n</sub> are given as follows:

n    |0&nbsp;|1&nbsp;|2&nbsp;|3&nbsp;|4&nbsp;|5&nbsp;|6&nbsp;|7&nbsp;|8&nbsp;|9&nbsp;|10|11|12|...
-----|--|--|--|--|--|--|--|--|--|--|--|--|--|---
$A_n$|0&nbsp;|1&nbsp;|2&nbsp;|3&nbsp;|4&nbsp;|5&nbsp;|6&nbsp;|9&nbsp;|10|11|12|13|18|...

We can also verify that A<sub>100</sub> = 3251 and A<sub>1000</sub> = 80852364498.

Find the last 9 digits of $\sum\_{k=1}^{18}A\_{10^k}$.
***
## **Dãy con Thue-Morse**

Dãy Thue-Morse: $T_0=0$, $T_{2n}=T_n$, $T_{2n+1}=1-T_n$. Đặt $b(0)=b(1)=1$, $b(2)=2$ và $b(n) = b(n - T_n) + b(n - T_{n-1})$ với các hệ số Thue-Morse.

Tính $b(10^{18}) \pmod{10^8+7}$.
