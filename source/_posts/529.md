**10-substrings**

A *10-substring* of a number is a substring of its digits that sum to 10. For example, the 10-substrings of the number 3523014 are:
- <b><u>352</u></b>3014
- 3<b><u>523</u></b>014
- 3<b><u>5230</u></b>14
- 35<b><u>23014</u></b>

A number is called *10-substring-friendly* if every one of its digits belongs to a 10-substring. For example, 3523014 is 10-substring-friendly, but 28546 is not.

Let T(n) be the number of 10-substring-friendly numbers from 1 to 10<sup>n</sup> (inclusive).
For example T(2) = 9 and T(5) = 3492.

Find T(10<sup>18</sup>) mod 1 000 000 007.
***
## **Chuỗi con $10$**

Đếm số nguyên $n$ chữ số ($n = 10^6$) chứa mỗi $10$ chữ số liên tiếp $0$-$9$ ít nhất một lần. Tính $f(10) \pmod{10^9}$.
