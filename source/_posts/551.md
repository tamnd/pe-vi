**Sum of digits sequence**

Let a<sub>0</sub>, a<sub>1</sub>, a<sub>2</sub>, ... be an integer sequence defined by:
- a<sub>0</sub> = 1;
- for n ≥ 1, a<sub>n</sub> is the sum of the digits of all preceding terms.

The sequence starts with 1, 1, 2, 4, 8, 16, 23, 28, 38, 49, ...
You are given a<sub>10<sup>6</sup></sub>&nbsp;=&nbsp;31054319.

Find a<sub>10<sup>15</sup></sub>.
***
## **Dãy tổng chữ số**

$a_0 = 1$, $a_n = a_{n-1} + S(a_{n-1})$ với $S(k)$ là tổng chữ số. Tính $a(10^{15})$.
