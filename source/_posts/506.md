**Clock sequence**

Consider the infinite repeating sequence of digits:

<center>1234321234321234321...</center>

Amazingly, you can break this sequence of digits into a sequence of integers such that the sum of the digits in the n'th value is n.

The sequence goes as follows:

<center>1, 2, 3, 4, 32, 123, 43, 2123, 432, 1234, 32123, ...</center>

Let v<sub>n</sub> be the n'th value in this sequence. For example, v<sub>2</sub>&nbsp;=&nbsp;2, v<sub>5</sub>&nbsp;=&nbsp;32 and v<sub>11</sub>&nbsp;=&nbsp;32123.

Let S(n) be v<sub>1</sub>&nbsp;+&nbsp;v<sub>2</sub>&nbsp;+&nbsp;...&nbsp;+&nbsp;v<sub>n</sub>. For example, S(11)&nbsp;=&nbsp;36120, and S(1000) mod 123454321 = 18232686.

Find S(10<sup>14</sup>)&nbsp;mod&nbsp;123454321.
***
## **Dãy đồng hồ**

Đồng hồ số $12$ giờ. Dãy số đọc từ đồng hồ. Tính $f(10^6)$.
