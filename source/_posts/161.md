<b>Triominoes</b>

A triomino is a shape consisting of three squares joined via the edges. There are two basic forms:

<center><img src="/resources/images/0161_trio1.gif"></center>

If all possible orientations are taken into account there are six:

<center><img src="/resources/images/0161_trio3.gif"></center>

Any n by m grid for which nxm is divisible by 3 can be tiled with triominoes.
If we consider tilings that can be obtained by reflection or rotation from another tiling as different there are 41 ways a 2 by 9 grid can be  tiled with triominoes:

<center><img src="/resources/images/0161_k9.gif"></center>

In how many ways can a 9 by 12 grid be tiled in this way by triominoes?
***
## **Triomino**

Có bao nhiêu cách lát lưới $9 \times 12$ bằng các triomino (xét phản chiếu và xoay là các cách khác nhau)?
