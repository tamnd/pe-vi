**A weird recurrence relation**

The function f is defined for all positive integers as follows:
- f(1)=1
- f(3)=3
- f(2n)=f(n)
- f(4n+1)=2f(2n+1)−f(n)
- f(4n+3)=3f(2n+1)−2f(n)

The function S(n) is defined as ∑<sub>i=1</sub><sup>n</sup>f(i).

S(8)=22 and S(100)=3604.

Find S(337). Give the last 9 digits of your answer.
***
## **Quy tắc truy hồi kỳ lạ**

$f(0)=0$, $f(2n-1)=f(n)$, $f(2n)=f(n)+f(n-1)+1$ với $n \ge 1$. Tính $\sum_{n=0}^{10^{15}} f(n) \pmod{10^9}$.
