# [Problem 337](https://projecteuler.net/problem=337)
***
**Totient Stairstep Sequences**

Let {a<sub>1</sub>, a<sub>2</sub>,..., a<sub>n</sub>} be an integer sequence of length n such that:
- a<sub>1</sub> = 6
- for all 1 ≤ i &lt; n : φ(a<sub>i</sub>) &lt; φ(a<sub>i+1</sub>) &lt; a<sub>i</sub> &lt; a<sub>i+1</sub> <sup>1</sup>

Let S(N) be the number of such sequences with a<sub>n</sub> ≤ N.
For example, S(10) = 4: {6}, {6, 8}, {6, 8, 9} and {6, 10}.
We can verify that S(100) = 482073668 and S(10 000) mod 10<sup>8</sup> = 73808307.

Find S(20 000 000) mod 10<sup>8</sup>.

<sup>1</sup> φ denotes <b>Euler's totient function</b>.
***
## **Dãy bậc thang totient**

Dãy $\{a_1,\ldots,a_n\}$: $a_1=6$, $\varphi(a_i) < \varphi(a_{i+1}) < a_i < a_{i+1}$.

$S(N)$ là số dãy như vậy với $a_n \le N$. Tính $S(10^{13})$.
