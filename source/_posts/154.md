<b>Exploring Pascal's pyramid</b>

A triangular pyramid is constructed using spherical balls so that each ball rests on exactly three balls of the next lower level.

<center><img src="/resources/images/0154_pyramid.png"></center>

Then, we calculate the number of paths leading from the apex to each position:

A path starts at the apex and progresses downwards to any of the three spheres directly below the current position.

Consequently, the number of paths to reach a certain position is the sum of the numbers immediately above it (depending on the position, there are up to three numbers above it).

The result is <em>Pascal's pyramid</em> and the numbers at each level n are the coefficients of the trinomial expansion 
(x + y + z)<sup>n</sup>.

How many coefficients in the expansion of (x + y + z)<sup>200000</sup> are multiples of 10<sup>12</sup>?
***
## **Khám phá tháp Pascal**

Tháp Pascal ba chiều dùng hệ số khai triển $(x+y+z)^n$.

Có bao nhiêu hệ số trong khai triển $(x+y+z)^{200000}$ là bội số của $10^{12}$?
