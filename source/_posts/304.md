**Primonacci**

For any positive integer n the function next_prime(n) returns the smallest prime p such that p&gt;n.

The sequence a(n) is defined by:
a(1)=next_prime(10<sup>14</sup>) and a(n)=next_prime(a(n-1)) for n&gt;1.

The fibonacci sequence f(n) is defined by: f(0)=0, f(1)=1 and f(n)=f(n-1)+f(n-2) for n&gt;1.

The sequence b(n) is defined as f(a(n)).

Find ∑b(n) for 1≤n≤100 000. Give your answer mod 1234567891011. 
***
## **Primonacci**

$a(1) = \text{next\_prime}(10^{14})$, $a(n) = \text{next\_prime}(a(n-1))$. $b(n) = F(a(n))$ là số Fibonacci thứ $a(n)$.

Tính $\sum_{n=1}^{100000} b(n) \pmod{10^9}$.
