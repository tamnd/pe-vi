## **Angular Bisector and Tangent 2** 

Given is an integer sided triangle $ABC$ with $BC \le AC \le AB$.<br/>
$k$ is the angular bisector of angle $ACB$.<br/>
$m$ is the tangent at $C$ to the circumscribed circle of $ABC$.<br/>
$n$ is a line parallel to $m$ through $B$.<br/>
The intersection of $n$ and $k$ is called $E$.

<div style="text-align:center"><img src="/resources/images/0296_bisector.gif" alt="0296_bisector.gif"></div>

How many triangles $ABC$ with a perimeter not exceeding $1\ 000\ 000$ exist such that $CE$ has integral length?

**Note**: This problem is a more difficult version of [Problem 296](https://projecteuler.net/problem=296). Please pay close attention to the differences between the two statements.

***
## **Đường phân giác và tiếp tuyến 2**

Biến thể bài 296. Đếm tam giác với chu vi $\le 10^8$.
