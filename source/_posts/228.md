**Minkowski Sums**

Let S<sub>n</sub> be the regular n-sided polygon – or <i>shape</i> – whose vertices v<sub>k</sub> (k&thinsp;=&thinsp;1,2,…,n) have coordinates:
<center>$x_k= \text{cos(}\frac{2k-1}{n} \times 180^\circ \text{)}$
$y_k= \text{sin(}\frac{2k-1}{n} \times 180^\circ \text{)}$
</center>

Each S<sub>n</sub> is to be interpreted as a filled shape consisting of all points on the perimeter and in the interior.

The <i>Minkowski sum</i>, S+T, of two shapes S and T is the result of adding every point in S to every point in T, where point addition is performed coordinate-wise: (u,&thinsp;v) + (x,&thinsp;y) = (u+x,&thinsp;v+y).

For example, the sum of S<sub>3</sub> and S<sub>4</sub> is the six-sided shape shown in pink below:

<center><img src="/resources/images/0228.png" alt="picture showing S_3 + S_4"></center>

How many sides does S<sub>1864</sub>&thinsp;+&thinsp;S<sub>1865</sub>&thinsp;+&thinsp;…&thinsp;+&thinsp;S<sub>1909</sub> have?

## **Tổng Minkowski**

Tổng Minkowski $S_m + S_n$ của đa giác đều $m$ cạnh và $n$ cạnh là đa giác bao lồi.

Với $3 \le m < n \le 1399$, có bao nhiêu cặp $(m,n)$ sao cho $S_m + S_n$ là đa giác đều?
