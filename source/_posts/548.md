**Gozinta Chains**

A <b>gozinta chain</b> for n is a sequence {1,a,b,...,n} where each element properly divides the next.
There are eight gozinta chains for 12:
{1,12} ,{1,2,12}, {1,2,4,12}, {1,2,6,12}, {1,3,12}, {1,3,6,12}, {1,4,12} and {1,6,12}. 
Let g(n) be the number of gozinta chains for n, so g(12)=8.
g(48)=48 and g(120)=132.

Find the sum of the numbers n not exceeding 10<sup>16</sup> for which g(n)=n.
***
## **Chuỗi Gozinta**

Chuỗi Gozinta của $n$: $1 = a_0, a_1, \ldots, a_k = n$ với $a_i | a_{i+1}$. $g(n)$ là số chuỗi. Tính $\sum_{n=1}^{10^6} g(n)$.
