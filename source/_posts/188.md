**The hyperexponentiation of a number**

The <span style="font-style: italic">hyperexponentiation</span> or <span style="font-style: italic">tetration</span> of a number a by a positive integer b, denoted by a↑↑b or <sup>b</sup>a, is recursively defined by:

a↑↑1 = a,
a↑↑(k+1) = a<sup>(a↑↑k)</sup>.

Thus we have e.g. 3↑↑2 = 3<sup>3</sup> = 27, hence 3↑↑3 = 3<sup>27</sup> = 7625597484987 and 3↑↑4 is roughly 10<sup>3.6383346400240996*10^12</sup>.

Find the last 8 digits of 1777↑↑1855.
***
## **Siêu lũy thừa**

Siêu lũy thừa $a \uparrow\uparrow b$ được định nghĩa đệ quy: $a\uparrow\uparrow 1 = a$, $a\uparrow\uparrow(k+1) = a^{(a\uparrow\uparrow k)}$.

Tìm $8$ chữ số cuối của $1777\uparrow\uparrow 1855$.
