**Counting primitive Pythagorean triples**

A <b>Pythagorean triple</b> consists of three positive integers $a$, $b$ and $c$ satsifying $a^2+b^2=c^2$.
The triple is called primitive if $a$, $b$ and $c$ are relatively prime.
Let P($n$) be the number of <b>primitive Pythagorean triples</b> with $a<b<c \le n$.
For example P(20) = 3, since there are three triples: (3,4,5), (5,12,13) and (8,15,17).

You are given that P(10<sup>6</sup>) = 159139.
Find P(3141592653589793).
***
## **Đếm bộ ba Pythagore nguyên thủy**

$T(N)$ là số bộ ba Pythagore nguyên thủy $(a,b,c)$ với $a < b < c \le N$. Tính $T(10^{16}) \pmod{10^9}$.
