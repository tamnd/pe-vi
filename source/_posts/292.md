**Pythagorean Polygons**

We shall define a <i>pythagorean polygon</i>  to be a <b>convex polygon</b> with the following properties:
- there are at least three vertices,
- no three vertices are aligned,
- each vertex has <b>integer coordinates</b>,
- each edge has <b>integer length</b>.

For a given integer n, define P(n) as the number of distinct pythagorean polygons for which the perimeter is ≤&thinsp;n.
Pythagorean polygons should be considered distinct as long as none is a translation of another.

You are given that P(4)&thinsp;=&thinsp;1, P(30)&thinsp;=&thinsp;3655 and P(60)&thinsp;=&thinsp;891045.
Find P(120).
***
## **Đa giác Pythagore**

Đa giác lồi $n$ đỉnh với tọa độ nguyên và mỗi cạnh có độ dài nguyên.

$P(n)$ là số đa giác Pythagore phân biệt có chu vi $\le n$. Tính $P(10^6)$.
