**Sums of totients of powers**
Let $\phi(n)$ be Euler's totient function.

Let $f(n)=(\Sigma^n_{i=1}\phi(n^i)) \text{ mod } (n+1)$.

Let $g(n)=\Sigma^n_{i=1}f(i)$.

$g(100)=2007$.

Find $g(5 \times 10^8)$.
***
## **Tổng totient lũy thừa**

$f(n) = \sum_{k=1}^{n} \varphi(k^5)$. Tính $f(10^{12}) \pmod{10^9}$.
