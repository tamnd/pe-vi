# [Problem 216](https://projecteuler.net/problem=216)
***
**Investigating the primality of numbers of the form 2n<sup>2</sup>-1**

Consider numbers t(n) of the form t(n) = 2n<sup>2</sup>-1 with n &gt; 1.
The first such numbers are 7, 17, 31, 49, 71, 97, 127 and 161.
It turns out that only 49 = 7\*7 and 161 = 7\*23 are not prime.
For n ≤ 10000 there are 2202 numbers t(n) that are prime.

How many numbers t(n) are prime for n ≤ 50,000,000 ?
***
## **Số nguyên tố dạng $2n^2-1$**

Xét $t(n) = 2n^2-1$ với $n > 1$. Có bao nhiêu giá trị $t(n)$ là số nguyên tố với $n \le 50000000$?
