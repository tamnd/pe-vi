## **Reciprocal Cycles**

A unit fraction contains $1$ in the numerator. The decimal representation of the unit fractions with denominators $2$ to $10$ are given:

$$\begin{aligned}
1/2 &= 0.5\\\\
1/3 &= 0.(3)\\\\
1/4 &= 0.25\\\\
1/5 &= 0.2\\\\
1/6 &= 0.1(6)\\\\
1/7 &= 0.(142857)\\\\
1/8 &= 0.125\\\\
1/9 &= 0.(1)\\\\
1/10 &= 0.1\\\\
\end{aligned}$$

Where $0.1(6)$ means $0.166666\ldots$, and has a $1$-digit recurring cycle. It can be seen that $1/7$ has a $6$-digit recurring cycle.

Find the value of $d<1000$ for which $1/d$ contains the longest recurring cycle in its decimal fraction part.

***
## **Chu kỳ phân số nghịch đảo**

Phân số đơn vị có tử số là $1$. $1/7 = 0.(142857)$ có chu kỳ lặp $6$ chữ số.

Tìm giá trị $d < 1000$ mà $1/d$ có chu kỳ lặp dài nhất trong phần thập phân.
