## **Addition Equations**

A string forms an <i>addition equation</i> if it consists of
- Digits, forming positive integers. No leading zeros are allowed.
- A single plus sign between integers.
- A single equality sign.

Most importantly, the equality must hold.

Example of strings of length $7$ forming addition equations:<span style='font-family:"Lucida Consolas", "Consolas", "Courier New", monospace'>
1+1+1=3
100=100
77=7+70
1+2=2+1</span>

Note that strings are considered different even if they form equivalent equations, so for example here are three unique strings:<span style='font-family:"Lucida Consolas", "Consolas", "Courier New", monospace'>
1+2=3
2+1=3
3=1+2</span>

The following strings do not form valid addition equations:<span style='font-family:"Lucida Consolas", "Consolas", "Courier New", monospace'>
1+1=3
1+1=02
0+1=1
+1=1
2-1=1</span>

Let $A(n)$ be the number of strings of length not larger than $n$ forming addition equations.

You are given $A(3) = 9$, $A(5)=171$ and $A(7)=4878$.

Find $A(50)$. Give your answer modulo $10^9 + 7$.

***
## **Phương trình cộng**

Đếm bộ $(a,b,c,d)$ với $a+b = c \times d$ và $a \times b = c + d$ và $a,b,c,d \le n$. Tính $f(10^6)$.
