**Arithmetic Derivative**

The <strong>arithmetic derivative</strong> is defined by
- p' = 1 for any prime p
- (ab)' = a'b + ab' for all integers a, b (Leibniz rule)

For example, 20' = 24.

Find ∑ <strong>gcd</strong>(k,k') for 1 &lt; k ≤ 5·10<sup>15</sup>.

Note: <strong>gcd</strong>(x,y) denotes the greatest common divisor of x and y.
***
## **Đạo hàm số học**

Đạo hàm số học: $(p)' = 1$ với $p$ nguyên tố; $(ab)' = a'b + ab'$. Tính $\sum_{n=1}^{10^{15}} n' \pmod{10^9}$.
