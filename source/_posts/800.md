## **Hybrid Integers**

An integer of the form $p^q q^p$ with prime numbers $p \neq q$ is called a <i>hybrid-integer</i>.
For example, $800 = 2^5 5^2$ is a hybrid-integer.

We define $C(n)$ to be the number of hybrid-integers less than or equal to $n$.<br />
You are given $C(800) = 2$ and $C(800^{800}) = 10790$.

Find $C(800800^{800800})$.

***
## **Số nguyên lai**

$n$ là số nguyên lai nếu $n = p^q \cdot q^p$ với $p < q$ nguyên tố. Đếm số nguyên lai $\le 800800$.
