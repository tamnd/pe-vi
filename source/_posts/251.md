**Cardano Triplets**

A triplet of positive integers (a,b,c) is called a Cardano Triplet if it satisfies the condition:

<center><img src="https://projecteuler.net/project/images/p251_cardano.gif" alt=""></center>

For example, (2,1,5) is a Cardano Triplet.

There exist 149 Cardano Triplets for which a+b+c ≤ 1000.

Find how many Cardano Triplets exist such that a+b+c ≤ 110,000,000.
***
## **Bộ ba Cardano**

Bộ ba nguyên dương $(a,b,c)$ gọi là bộ ba Cardano nếu $\sqrt[3]{a + b\sqrt{c}} + \sqrt[3]{a - b\sqrt{c}} = 1$.

Có bao nhiêu bộ ba Cardano với $a + b + c \le 110000000$?
