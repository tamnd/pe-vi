**Faulhaber's Formulas**

The sum of the k<sup>th</sup> powers of the first n positive integers can be expressed as a polynomial of degree k+1 with rational coefficients, the <b>Faulhaber's Formulas</b>:
$$1^k + 2^k + ... + n^k = \sum_{i=1}^n i^k = \sum_{i=1}^{k+1} a_{i} n^i = a_{1} n + a_{2} n^2 + ... + a_{k} n^k + a_{k+1} n^{k + 1},$$
where a<sub>i</sub>'s are rational coefficients that can be written as reduced fractions p<sub>i</sub>/q<sub>i</sub> (if a<sub>i</sub>&nbsp;=&nbsp;0, we shall consider q<sub>i</sub>&nbsp;=&nbsp;1).

For example, $1^4 + 2^4 + ... + n^4 = -\frac{1}{30}n + \frac{1}{3}n^3 + \frac{1}{2}n^4 + \frac{1}{5}n^5$.

Define D(k) as the value of q<sub>1</sub> for the sum of k<sup>th</sup> powers (i.e. the denominator of the reduced fraction a<sub>1</sub>).
Define F(m) as the m<sup>th</sup> value of k&nbsp;≥&nbsp;1 for which D(k)&nbsp;=&nbsp;20010.
You are given D(4) = 30 (since a<sub>1</sub>&nbsp;=&nbsp;-1/30), D(308)&nbsp;=&nbsp;20010, F(1)&nbsp;=&nbsp;308, F(10)&nbsp;=&nbsp;96404.

Find F(10<sup>5</sup>).
***
## **Công thức Faulhaber**

$S_e(n) = \sum_{k=1}^n k^e$. Đặt $f(e, p) = S_e(p-1) \bmod p$ với $p$ nguyên tố. Tính $\sum_{e=1}^{10^4} \sum_{p \le 10^3} f(e,p)$.
